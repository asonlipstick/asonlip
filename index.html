About Me

Site Reliability Engineer & Cloud Infrastructure Specialist

I'm a Site Reliability Engineer dedicated to building and maintaining highly reliable, scalable cloud infrastructure. I focus on ensuring systems are available, performant, and resilient to failures.

My expertise includes designing SLIs and SLOs, implementing comprehensive monitoring and observability solutions, managing incidents, and automating toil to improve system reliability. I work with Kubernetes, cloud platforms, and modern observability tools to keep systems running smoothly.

I believe in the SRE principles of balancing reliability and feature velocity, using error budgets, and continuously improving system resilience through automation, monitoring, and post-incident analysis.
